Project Overview

Business Case

Our customers will be any group of people who need to decide on a place to eat, including students, group of full-time workers, a couple, a family, etc. These groups of people often find themselves not being able to reach an agreement on where to go eat, often consuming large amounts of time and ending up to the same default place. Our app would help them find a place to eat in a fast, efficient and fun way.

Currently there are services that allow people to find places to eat around them, but are usually tailored for a single person to simply find a new place. Our app takes decision making to the next level by allowing groups of people to find and decide on places together. Instead of one individual searching for a place to eat, it encourages each member to join in the search. As well as providing a commonly used search engine to find locations rather than having to use only locations known by the group.
